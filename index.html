About The Project

With the corporate culture at a rise and crunch time lying ahead for all of us, it is the need of the hour to make our life as user friendly and tech savy as possible. With this project we three are trying to do the same. Ever imagine sitting in your office and trying to directly connect to the devices at your home, like maybe switch on your AC, start your washing machine to clean up that dirty laundry left behind or maybe simply log in your home PC without manually switching it on. Now if this doesn't intice you we have more, it can be done by a few simple clicks on our website, all you need is to create an account and have an arduino microcontroller and leave the rest to us.

  • Custom Design

    Customize the arduino to fit your needs and lifestyle. Custom fit it with attachments of your choice.

  • Inovative Ideas

    Come up with your own creative ideas to control the arduino in any way you want to make your life more comfortable.

  • Secure and Safe

    Secure access to your arduino board with secure authentication and keychain protection.
